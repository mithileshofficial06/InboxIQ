## 📬 Core Features

### 1. Gmail OAuth Login
- Sign in with Google, request Gmail read permissions
- No password stored, fully secure

### 2. Inbox Dashboard (The Google Photos moment)
- Visual grid/card view of emails instead of boring list
- Filter by — People, Date, Category, Sentiment
- **Timeline view** — scroll through your email history like a photo timeline

### 3. Smart Auto-Categories (AI powered)
Automatically buckets every email into:
- 🧾 Bills & Invoices
- 💼 Job Applications & Responses
- 📦 Orders & Deliveries
- 🔔 OTPs & Notifications
- 📰 Newsletters
- 👤 Real people (actual human conversations)
- 🎓 College / Academic

### 4. People Intelligence
- Who emails you most?
- Who do you never reply to?
- Relationship strength score per contact
- Full conversation history per person in one clean view

### 5. Subscription Tracker
- Auto-detects every service you've signed up for
- Shows last email date, frequency
- Flags dead subscriptions (signed up, never heard from again)

### 6. Job Application Tracker *(huge for you right now)*
- Auto-detects application confirmation emails
- Tracks status — Applied → Replied → Interview → Offer → Rejected
- Timeline per company

### 7. AI Chat over your Inbox (RAG)
Natural language queries like:
- *"Do I have any unpaid invoices?"*
- *"When did I last hear from [person]?"*
- *"Which companies rejected me in 2024?"*
- *"Summarize everything from Swiggy this month"*

This is the **technically heavy part** — every email gets embedded as vectors in pgvector, AI retrieves relevant ones and answers

### 8. Insights & Stats
- Most active email day/time
- Average reply time
- Sentiment trend over months (are people emailing you positively or negatively?)
- Inbox growth over time

### 9. Memory / Highlights *(the Google Photos "3 years ago" feature)*
- *"1 year ago today you received your first internship offer"*
- *"This week in 2023 you applied to 5 companies"*
- Surfaces meaningful emails you forgot existed

---

## 🔒 Privacy First (important selling point)
- Emails never stored as raw text — only embeddings + metadata
- User can delete all their data anytime
- No emails sent to third parties
