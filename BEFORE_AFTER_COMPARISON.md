## 🏆 Design Philosophy

### **Magazine Editorial Style**
Inspired by high-end print magazines:
- Wide margins (48px container padding)
- Generous white space (40px gaps)
- Large typography (16-20px)
- Premium interactions (8px hover lift)
- Clean separators (2px borders)

### **Content-First Approach**
- **Focus**: Email content, not UI chrome
- **Breathing Room**: Every element has space
- **Hierarchy**: Clear visual importance
- **Simplicity**: No unnecessary decoration

### **Professional Polish**
- **Smooth Transitions**: 300ms ease-out
- **Consistent Spacing**: Mathematical rhythm
- **Thoughtful Colors**: Subtle, not loud
- **Quality Details**: Thick borders, big shadows
